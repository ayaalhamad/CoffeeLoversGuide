🌍 Trip Itinerary: Discovering Turkey

Welcome to the detailed breakdown of our unforgettable journey through Turkey! Here's a day-by-day summary of the trip highlights:

🗓 Day 1 – Arrival in Istanbul

  • Arrived at Istanbul Airport
  • Checked into the hotel in Sultanahmet
  • Evening walk along the Bosphorus
  • Welcome dinner with traditional Turkish mezes

🗓 Day 2 – Historical Gems of Istanbul

  • Visited the Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia
  • Lunch near the Basilica Cistern
  • Explored the Grand Bazaar and bought souvenirs
  • Sunset ferry ride on the Bosphorus

🗓 Day 3 – Flight to Cappadocia

  • Morning flight to Kayseri Airport
  • Arrived in Göreme, checked into a cave hotel
  • Visited Göreme Open Air Museum
  • Evening wine tasting at a local vineyard

🗓 Day 4 – Hot Air Balloon Adventure

  • Early morning hot air balloon ride over the fairy chimneys
  • Breakfast with a view
  • Hike through Ihlara Valley
  • Sunset at Uçhisar Castle

🗓 Day 5 – Back to Istanbul & Departure

  • Return flight to Istanbul
  • Last-minute shopping at Spice Bazaar
  • Farewell Turkish coffee by the Galata Tower
  • Departure from Istanbul Airport

✈️ Total Trip Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

🏨 Cities Visited: Istanbul, Cappadocia (Göreme)
